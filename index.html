Vegan Lasagna

Indulge in Layers of Delight: Try Our Exquisite Vegetable Lasagna Recipe!

by Lut


Servings: 4 Servings   Prep Time: 30 Mins   Cook Time: 40 Mins

Ingredients for Lasagna

  • 16 lasagna sheets
  • 1 ½ tablespoon olive oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large eggplant
  • ¾ teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • ½ cup of water
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Sweet Ground Red Pepper

Ingredients for Béchamel Sauce

  • 3 tbsp of Olive Oil
  • 500 ml Herbal Milk (can be soy milk)
  • 3 tbsp of Flour
  • ¾ teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions for Sautéed Vegetable Mix

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a generously sized pot or pan, ensuring it has a secure lid.
  3. Finely chop 2 cloves of garlic and incorporate them into the heated pan. Sauté the garlic for approximately 1 to 2 minutes
  4. Slice the zucchini, eggplant, and colored pepper into uniform pieces. Introduce the sliced vegetables to the pan and thoroughly combine.
  5. Pour 1 glass of tomato puree into the pan, along with 1 large tomato that has been diced. Additionally, include half a glass of hot water.
  6. Season the mixture with salt and all the desired spices. Thoroughly combine the ingredients, ensuring they are evenly distributed. Cover the pan with the lid and allow it to cook. Periodically monitor the cooking progress, adding water as necessary to prevent sticking to the bottom.

Instructions for Béchamel Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil.
  2. Incorporate 3 tablespoons of flour into the pot and cook while stirring until the aroma of the flour is released
  3. The resulting mixture should possess a slightly fluid consistency; if it appears lumpy like dough, it indicates inadequate oil content.
  4. Gradually pour vegetable milk into the cooked flour-oil mixture, whisking diligently with a wire whisk to prevent the formation of lumps.
  5. Once all the milk has been added, season with salt and pepper as desired.
  6. Continue cooking the béchamel sauce until it achieves a thickened consistency.
  7. It should not solidify completely, as it will undergo further cooking in the oven.
  8. In case the sauce loses its fluidity, incorporate a small amount of additional milk.

Instructions for Lasagna

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Each lasagna sheet will be soaked in this boiling water for 1 minute.
  2. Apply a thin layer of olive oil to the bottom of a deep baking tray, approximately the size of 3 lasagna sheets.
  3. Arrange a layer of lasagna sheets that have been soaked in water on the tray. Spread one-third of the vegetable mixture evenly over the sheets. Follow with a quarter of the béchamel sauce.
  4. Repeat the layering process by adding another layer of lasagna sheets. For the final layer, distribute the remaining vegetables, followed by the last portion of béchamel sauce.
  5. Place tomato slices on top of the lasagna layers. Transfer the baking tray to the oven.
  6. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 40-45 minutes or until the lasagna achieves a well-browned appearance.
